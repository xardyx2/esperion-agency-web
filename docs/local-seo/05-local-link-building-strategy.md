## 11. Link Building Don'ts

### ❌ AVOID

1. **Buying Links**
   - Against Google guidelines
   - Risk of penalty
   - Wasted money

2. **PBN (Private Blog Networks)**
   - Easy to detect
   - High risk
   - Short-term gains only

3. **Link Exchanges (Excessive)**
   - "I link to you, you link to me" pattern
   - Detectable by algorithms
   - Devalued by Google

4. **Low-Quality Directories**
   - Spammy link farms
   - No editorial review
   - Can hurt rankings

5. **Irrelevant Links**
   - Links from unrelated sites
   - Low value
   - Looks unnatural

6. **Exact Match Anchor Text (Overuse)**
   - Too many "digital agency jakarta" anchors
   - Unnatural pattern
   - Trigger Penguin penalty
