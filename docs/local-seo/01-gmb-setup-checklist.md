## 10. Common Mistakes to AVOID

### ❌ DO NOT:
- Use keyword-stuffed business name (e.g., "Esperion Digital Agency Jakarta SEO Web Mobile")
- Create multiple listings for same location
- Use fake reviews
- Ignore negative reviews
- Post inconsistently
- Use stock photos
- Copy competitor descriptions
- Change NAP frequently

### ✅ DO:
- Keep NAP 100% consistent everywhere
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- Post weekly (minimum)
- Upload authentic photos
- Encourage real customer reviews
- Update hours during holidays
- Use relevant keywords naturally
- Monitor insights regularly
