## 1. Review Generation Strategy

### When to Ask for Reviews

| Trigger Event | Timing | Method |
|--------------|--------|--------|
| Project completion | Within 3-5 days | Email + WhatsApp |
| Positive feedback received | Immediately | Reply + Ask |
| Monthly check-in (retainer clients) | End of month | Email |
| After successful milestone | Same day | WhatsApp |
| Post-support resolution (if positive) | After confirmation | SMS/Email |

### Review Target Goals

| Platform | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 | Year 1 |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|--------|
| Google | 5 | 15 | 30 | 50+ |
| Facebook | 3 | 10 | 20 | 35+ |

| Clutch.co | 1 | 3 | 5 | 10+ |
| **Total** | 11 | 33 | 65 | 110+ |

## 3. QR Code Setup

### Generate QR Code for Google Review

1. Get your Google Review link:
   - Go to Google Maps
   - Search your business
   - Click "Write a review"
   - Copy URL or use shortened version

2. Create QR Code:
   - Use QR code generator (qr-code-generator.com, qrstuff.com)
   - Paste review link
   - Customize with logo/colors
   - Download high-res version

### QR Code Usage

| Material | Placement |
|----------|-----------|
| Business cards | Back of card |
| Email signature | Below contact info |
| Invoice/Receipt | Footer |
| Thank you cards | Center |
| Presentation deck | Last slide |
| Office signage | Reception area |
| Digital proposals | Last page |

## 7. Incentives (What's Allowed)

### ✅ ALLOWED
- Asking for honest reviews
- Providing easy links/QR codes
- Thanking reviewers
- Featuring reviews in marketing materials
- Sending reminders

### ❌ NOT ALLOWED (Against TOS)
- Offering money/gifts for reviews
- Exchanging reviews (you review me, I review you)
- Posting fake reviews
- Review gating (only asking happy customers)
- Incentivizing only positive reviews
- Using review stations at your location

## 9. Crisis Management

### If Negative Review Goes Viral

1. **Immediate (Within 2 hours)**
   - Acknowledge publicly
   - Show empathy
   - Provide contact info for resolution

2. **Within 24 hours**
   - Contact reviewer privately
   - Understand the issue
   - Propose solution

3. **Resolution**
   - Fix the problem
   - Ask if they'd consider updating review
   - Document lessons learned

4. **Post-Resolution**
   - Respond publicly thanking them for working together
   - Share improvements made
   - Update team on learnings
